Project Name
Fleeting Moments
Author
Jordy Schoolmeesters
Medium
Web, Interactive
Tech Stack
HTML, Tailwind CSS, p5.js

Fleeting Moments is an interactive reflection on how digital memories lose meaning through their volume and velocity. Moments gradually distort and dissolve, mirroring how our memories fade when everything is stored without intention or recollection.

Give it a try, upload either 4 or 16 JPEG images and see what happens.
